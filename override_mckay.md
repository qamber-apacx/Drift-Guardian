## OVERRIDE 001 — After-Hours Break Reduction

**Control:** AFTER_HOURS_BREAK
**Original Policy Requirement:** 8-hour break between end of work and start of
next working day.
**Override Value:** 6-hour break is approved for the duration of the 2024
infrastructure maintenance season (1 March 2024 to 31 October 2024).

**Reason:** Increased callout volume during the infrastructure upgrade contracts
with Vector and Transpower requires reduced minimum break times during peak
periods. Fatigue management plan approved by Health & Safety Committee on
12 February 2024 must be in place.

**Approved by:** CEO
**Reference:** HSC-2024-001

## OVERRIDE 002 — Timesheet Submission Window

**Control:** DAILY_TIMESHEET_SUBMISSION
**Original Policy Requirement:** Daily timesheets due by 8:30 am the following
day.
**Override Value:** 10:00 am submission deadline approved for field technicians
on multi-site jobs only.

**Reason:** Field technicians completing multi-site runs cannot reasonably
complete and submit timesheets by 8:30 am due to travel constraints. The
extended window applies only to field technicians. Office-based and depot staff
retain the 8:30 am deadline.

**Approved by:** CEO and COO
**Reference:** OPS-2024-007

---

## OVERRIDE 003 — Weekly Timesheet Submission Window

**Control:** WEEKLY_TIMESHEET_SUBMISSION
**Original Policy Requirement:** Weekly timesheets due by 8:30 am the following
Monday.
**Override Value:** 10:00 am Monday submission deadline approved for field
technicians on multi-site jobs only (same scope as Override 002).

**Approved by:** CEO and COO
**Reference:** OPS-2024-007
