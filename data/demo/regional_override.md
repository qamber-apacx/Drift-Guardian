# Regional Override — United Kingdom (FCA)

This regional override is the **higher authority** for the requirements it
covers. Where it specifies a value, that value REPLACES the global policy. Any
requirement not mentioned here inherits the global policy unchanged.

## R-1 Suspicious Activity Reporting (supersedes Global 3.1)
A Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) must be filed **within 15 days** of detection.

## R-2 Risk Rating (supersedes Global 4.1)
Every customer's risk rating must be reviewed **at least every 12 months**.

## R-3 Politically Exposed Persons (supersedes Global 6.1)
A PEP may only be onboarded after **explicit senior-management approval**, in
addition to Enhanced Due Diligence.
