# SOP: Customer Onboarding & Monitoring (PASS example)

Use with: global_policy.md + regional_override.md.
Expected verdict: **PASS** — every effective requirement is honoured.

## Onboarding
- We confirm the customer's identity before the account is switched on, capturing
  legal name, date of birth, address, and a government photo ID.
- High-risk customers go through Enhanced Due Diligence, including a recorded
  check of where their funds come from, before approval.
- Politically Exposed Persons are onboarded only after senior management signs
  off, on top of Enhanced Due Diligence.

## Monitoring & Reporting
- Suspicious activity is reported within 15 days of being spotted.
- Each customer's risk rating is reviewed at least once every 12 months.

## Records
- KYC records are kept for 7 years after an account closes.
- Pulling up a customer's KYC records requires two-factor authentication.
