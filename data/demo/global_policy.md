# Global KYC / AML Policy (Authoritative)

This is the master compliance policy. It applies to every region unless a
regional override explicitly supersedes a specific requirement.

## 1.0 Customer Due Diligence (CDD)
1.1 Customer identity must be verified **before** an account is activated.
1.2 Verification must capture full legal name, date of birth, residential
    address, and a government-issued photo ID.

## 2.0 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
2.1 High-risk customers require Enhanced Due Diligence, including a documented
    **source-of-funds** check before the account is approved.

## 3.0 Suspicious Activity Reporting
3.1 A Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) must be filed **within 30 days** of the
    activity being detected.

## 4.0 Risk Rating
4.1 Every customer's risk rating must be reviewed **at least every 24 months**.

## 5.0 Record Keeping
5.1 KYC records must be **retained for 7 years** after account closure.
5.2 Access to KYC records requires **two-factor authentication**.

## 6.0 Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
6.1 PEPs may be onboarded following standard Enhanced Due Diligence.
