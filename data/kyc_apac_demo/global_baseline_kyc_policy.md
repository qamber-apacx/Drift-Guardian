# Global Baseline KYC Policy

Authoritative group-wide baseline for customer onboarding. Applies to every
region unless a regional bundle explicitly supersedes a specific control.

## 1. Risk Scoring & Enhanced Due Diligence
1.1 Each customer is assigned an automated risk score from 0 to 100.
1.2 Any customer whose risk score is **80 or above** must undergo Enhanced Due
    Diligence (EDD) before the account is opened. This is the group escalation
    threshold; regional bundles may set their own approved threshold.

## 2. Approval Authority
2.1 Onboarding of an EDD-flagged customer must be approved by the **Money
    Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO)**. Approval authority may be reassigned
    only by a regional bundle, never by a local procedure.

## 3. Pre-Approval Control
3.1 EDD-flagged customers require **senior pre-approval BEFORE onboarding**. The
    account must not be opened until that approval is recorded.

## 4. Escalation Review Time
4.1 An escalated EDD case must receive a documented review decision **within 24
    hours** of escalation.

## 5. Record Keeping
5.1 KYC records are retained for 7 years after account closure.
