# SOP: APAC Customer Onboarding (AI-generated draft)

Operational procedure for onboarding customers in the APAC region.

## 1. Risk Assessment
Each applicant receives an automated risk score. Applicants who score **90 or
above** are routed into the Enhanced Due Diligence workflow; applicants below
that score proceed on the standard path.

## 2. Enhanced Due Diligence Handling
For applicants in the Enhanced Due Diligence workflow, the **onboarding agent**
reviews the case and authorises the account to be opened.

## 3. Opening & Review
The account is opened once the applicant's documents are collected. The Enhanced
Due Diligence case is then logged as a **post-onboarding compliance checkpoint**
and reviewed within **72 hours** of being raised.

## 4. Records
Customer KYC records are retained for 7 years after the account is closed.
